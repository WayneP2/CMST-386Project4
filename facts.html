Breast Cancer Facts

Fact #1

1 in 8 women develop breast cancer in the US

Fact #2

There are over 3.8 million breast cancer survivors in the United States

Fact #3

There are 4 stages of breast cancer. Stage 4 means that the cancer has spread to other parts of the body.
