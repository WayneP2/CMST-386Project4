Symptoms

    Tenderness in the nipple or lump near the breast or underarm area.
  • Change in skin texture or enlargement of the pores of the breast skin
  • Lump in the breast. Should be examined by medical professional, however, not necessarily a guarantee of a being positive for breast cancer.

All symptoms should be verified by a medical professional to ensure a positive or negative test for breast cancer.
